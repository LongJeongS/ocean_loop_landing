About Ocean Loop

What is Ocean Loop?
Ocean Loop is the direct-to-consumer service launched by OV Loop to support Fisker Ocean owners in North America and Europe. It provides cloud connectivity, mobile network access, over-the-air (OTA) software updates, remote diagnostics and repair software, and future feature upgrades — ensuring vehicles remain functional, secure, and continuously improving even after Fisker Inc.'s closure.
Who is OV Loop?
OV Loop is a software provider with deep expertise in digital payments infrastructure, automotive, digital wallets, and mobility network platforms. OV Loop was hired by American Lease and the Fisker bankruptcy trustee to manage and maintain the connected cloud infrastructure for Fisker Ocean vehicles.
What happened to Fisker Inc.?
Fisker Inc. is no longer operational. Fisker filed for bankruptcy in June 2024 due to financial difficulties, entering Chapter 7 bankruptcy leading to the liquidation of the company and its assets. Ocean Loop exists specifically to ensure Fisker Ocean owners continue to receive software support.
Is Ocean Loop officially sanctioned?
Yes. OV Loop was contracted by American Lease and the Fisker bankruptcy trustee to manage the connected cloud infrastructure for Fisker Ocean vehicles.

Subscription & Pricing

What's included in the base subscription?
The $499/year subscription includes: cloud connectivity, OTA updates through 2.2.3, remote diagnostics and fixes, the new secure O Loop mobile app, streaming/music access, GPS/time accuracy, PKC (Phone-as-a-Key) support, and phone/email customer support.
Will OS 2.5 and 2.6 be included in my base subscription?
No. OS 2.5 and 2.6 are optional add-ons priced separately, you can add them during signup or later from your profile under Plan → Edit options.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Your vehicle keeps all installed updates after cancellation, but you'll lose cloud access, support, and eligibility for future OTA updates.
Will features be removed if I unsubscribe?
No. Updates already installed on your vehicle remain active. What you lose is cloud connectivity, the O Loop app access, remote diagnostics, support, and eligibility for future updates.
Will future updates cost extra?
Yes. Future OS versions beyond 2.6 will be optional add-ons priced separately from the base subscription. Pricing will always be communicated in advance.

OS Updates

What software version will I receive when I sign up?
You will receive software versions up to 2.2.3. After activation, OS 2.2.1 → 2.2.2 → 2.2.3 are delivered sequentially. T-Mobile and cloud connectivity setup takes 24–48 business hours, then the 2.2.3 OTA typically follows within 7–10 business days.
What's the difference between OS 2.5 and 2.6?
OS 2.5 delivers major infotainment, performance, and UI upgrades via OTA with no additional hardware. OS 2.6 builds on 2.5 by adding Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support, but requires a USB hardware kit and can only be installed via OTA. It introduces phone mirroring, voice assistant support, and deeper media integration.
Is there a 2.4 update?
No. You go directly from OS 2.2.3 to OS 2.5.
Do OS 2.5 and 2.6 apply to European vehicles?
Yes. The features and timeline for 2.5 and 2.6 apply to both North American and European vehicles.
Can I drive while the update is downloading?
Yes. Your Ocean is safe to drive while the update downloads in the background. However, once you initiate the installation, the vehicle cannot be driven. Allow approximately 1 hour for installation to complete while the vehicle remains stationary.
I paid for OS 2.5 — when will the icon appear?
Please allow 2–3 business days for the download/update icon to appear on your Ocean display after payment is confirmed. If you don't see it after 3 business days, contact our support team.
What if the OTA update fails?
You may be referred to a certified FAST partner for an in-shop update. Some vehicles may not support OTA updates due to hardware configurations — vehicle conditioning (healthy 12V battery, good connectivity) before the update is important.

The O Loop App

What does the O Loop app do?
The O Loop app provides secure remote unlock/start, OTA status monitoring, vehicle status, Phone-as-Key (PKC), battery and range status, cabin preconditioning, California Mode, and location tracking. It is included with your subscription.
Are third-party tools like OceanLink Pro supported?
No. Legacy third-party APIs have been disabled for security reasons. Only the official O Loop mobile app is supported for vehicle access and control.

Security & Data

Is my data secure?
Yes. OV Loop uses encrypted data channels and secured app protocols across all systems — from software updates and personal information to remote vehicle access.
Can I opt out of updates or roll back software?
You may request to delay certain non-critical updates. However, once an update is installed, it cannot be rolled back.

App Troubleshooting

Most issues are resolved by reinstalling the latest version of the app.

⚡ Quick fix — works for most issues

1Uninstall the O Loop app.
2Reinstall the latest version from the App Store or Google Play.
3Open O Loop and sign in. If the password reset email doesn't work, use "Forgot password" directly in the app.
Password reset link doesn't work
Uninstall → reinstall latest version → use "Forgot password" directly in the app (don't use the email link).
App still shows "Link my EV" after pairing
Uninstall → reinstall latest version → sign in again.
Some commands don't run
Another app (like OceanLink Pro) may be connected to your Ocean. Force close both OceanLink Pro and O Loop, then open O Loop only.
Bluetooth (BLE) won't connect or is unstable
Try in order: (1) Force close and relaunch O Loop. (2) Confirm your Ocean is running OS 2.2.3. (3) Ensure Bluetooth is ON and O Loop has the right permissions (on Android: Location and Nearby devices). (4) Make sure no other app is connected via BLE to your Ocean. (5) Move away from clusters of other BLE devices before retrying.
